# Product Roadmap — Robinson R44 Raven II e-Learning

## Phase 1 — Discovery & Definition
- Define target learners and training org needs
- Align scope with SME input and regulatory expectations
- Confirm module outline and assessment approach

## Phase 2 — MVP Build
- Produce initial modules + interactions
- Implement knowledge checks and question bank structure
- Internal reviews with SMEs + iterative refinement

## Phase 3 — Pilot Release
- Release to selected stakeholders / training org(s)
- Collect feedback on usability, clarity, pacing, and assessments
- Fix high-priority gaps and improve learning flow

## Phase 4 — Full Release & Iteration
- Full rollout
- Track feedback, completion signals, and content improvements
- Plan enhancements (analytics, adaptive learning, expansions)
